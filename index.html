Who We Are

We are not a shelter, but instead a location that helps senior dogs
find a home without ever being euthanized. We care for every
single dog until we find someone who is willing to take on a new loving pet.

Our mission is to make sure they get a nice home before leaving us to cross the rainbow bridge.

Why Older Dogs Deserve A Loving Home

Senior dogs are often the ones last to get adopted, and at risk to get
euthanized at shelters.They spend 4 times longer than younger
dogs, having a 25% adoption rate, compared to the 60% of younger dogs.
When people go to shelters, they often look for younger dogs, because
of the life span and no pre-existing health problems compared to
senior dogs. Although senior dogs can come with challenges, they
have many more benefits! They are typically more gentle, have some training, and
potentially have minimal or no conflicts to any pets you could already own.
But most importantly, you would be saving a life, and giving them the chance
to live a better life before leaving. You are their last hope, and their last
love they will have on this earth. Give them a chance.

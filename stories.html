Their Stories.

Below you can read some heart-wrenching stories from
the dogs residing at H.R.O.D. You can also see their
availability for adoption!

BUTCH

Butch was found living close to a railway.
Someone called us to report about butch
not wanting to get up and seeing like it
was starving. Upon arrival, it was seen that
butch not only was straving and weak. But
also had a major infection on his back leg.
But now, he's ready to find a loving and
caring home!

GINGER

Ginger was dropped off at our location.
Someone tied ginger to our front bike
rack with a note tied to her collar. Upon
reading the note, it was discovered that
ginger's owner had passed away, and she
had no one to go to. Now she patiently
waits her new home!

ROOFUS

Roofus was taken from an abusive "home,"
after animal control was called on his
past owners. His past owners kept him
chained up, outside, and was barely feed
for most of his life. He was even found
with cuts along his body. We found roofus
at Barc, and told the organization we wanted
to give him an opportunity to find a great
home. Now he's excitedly waiting for his
new loving owners!

NIA

Nia was dropped off at our location after
their owner mentioned that they were
moving. They said they couldn't bring
along their dog with them, and that they
were trying to find a "shelter" that would
take in Nia. Nia is now looking for her
FOREVER and LOVING home!
